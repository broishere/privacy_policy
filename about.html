About

Shop9 is vision to bring local sellers and buyers online in order to build strong tightly knit community

In today's world, local sellers have to work hard to increase their reach with customers due to fierce competition from big players; while customers still not aware where could he/she get certain product in nearest shop. This problem is very big in small towns like Katol

Shop9 is solution to both sellers and customers

Sellers can start creating their online shops at NO COST (No direct and hidden cost) And customers can either buy products or enquire about these services online
